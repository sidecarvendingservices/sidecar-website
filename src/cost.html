Common Questions

Cost, answered.

Is there really no cost at all to my business?

Correct — no cost for the machine, installation, stocking, or maintenance. Sidecar's revenue comes from the purchases customers make at the machine, not from any fee charged to your business.

If it's free for us, how does Sidecar make money?

The same way any vending operator does — a small margin on each purchase made at the machine. That's the entire business model, which is why there's no separate charge to the location hosting it.

Do we pay for the electricity the machine uses?

Machines plug into a standard outlet and use a negligible amount of power — comparable to a small appliance. It isn't separately billed. If you have questions about your specific setup, we're happy to walk through it during your free on-site evaluation.

Is there a fee if we need to relocate or cancel?

No, and there's no minimum term either. The service agreement simply asks for 30 days' notice if you ever need a machine relocated or removed — whether that's next year or next month — with no cancellation fee or penalty involved.

Are there any hidden fees I should know about?

No. What's on this page is the complete picture: no cost for the machine, installation, stocking, or maintenance. If a specific question about your space comes up, we'll answer it plainly during your free evaluation before anything is installed.
