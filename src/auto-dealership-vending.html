Common Questions

Dealership vending, answered.

Can you place machines in both the showroom and the service bay?

Yes — many dealerships want both a customer-facing machine and one for technicians and staff. We'll size the setup to your floor plan during your free evaluation.

Is there a cost to our dealership?

No — installation, the machines, stocking, and maintenance are all included at no cost to you.

What if a machine goes down during a busy service day?

A local Metro Atlanta technician responds the same day, so waiting customers aren't looking at an out-of-order machine for long.

Can the product mix differ between the showroom and service bay?

Yes, we can tailor each machine's stock separately — customer-facing snacks and drinks up front, and options geared toward staff in the service area.
