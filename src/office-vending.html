Common Questions

Office vending, answered.

Is there really no cost to install a vending machine in our office?

Correct — installation, the machine itself, stocking, and ongoing maintenance are all covered by Sidecar. There's no upfront cost and no capital outlay required from your business.

Do we get to choose what's stocked?

Yes. During your free on-site evaluation, we discuss your team's preferences and build a starting product mix, then adjust over time based on what actually sells using our smart inventory monitoring.

What if the machine has a problem?

Call us and a local Metro Atlanta technician responds the same day — not a national call center. Repairs are included as part of the service.

How much space does a machine need?

Most office machines fit in a break room or a small nook near high foot traffic. We'll recommend the right size and placement during your free evaluation based on your actual space.
