The Fine Print

What happens to your current contract?

Most vending agreements run on a required notice period rather than a hard lock-in — check your current contract for what's needed (30, 60, and 90 days are all common), then reach out to us with your timeline. We'll build your installation date around it, so there's no gap where your space is left without a machine at all, and no need to manage two vendors at once.
