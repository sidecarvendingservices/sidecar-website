Privacy Policy

How Sidecar Vending Services LLC collects, uses, and protects information from the businesses we partner with and the people who use our machines.

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

Sidecar Vending Services LLC

Sidecar Vending Services LLC is a vending machine services company registered in the state of Georgia, serving North Metro Atlanta. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and our partner relationships, how we use it, and how you can control it.

What We Collect

Information we gather.

When a business requests a free evaluation through this website, we collect basic contact details (name, email, phone number, company, and location) through our online form. This information is stored in HubSpot, the customer relationship platform we use to manage partner communication.

As part of servicing a location, we may also collect basic employee contact information from partner businesses (such as names and emails of on-site staff or facility contacts) so we can coordinate installation, restocking, and support. This information is likewise stored in HubSpot.

How We Use It

What it's used for.

We use the information we collect to respond to evaluation requests, schedule and manage installations, provide ongoing service and support, and — unless you opt out — to send occasional marketing communications about Sidecar, such as updates or offers relevant to our vending service. We do not sell contact information to third parties.

Your Choices

Opting out or requesting removal.

If you'd rather not receive marketing communications from us, just let us know and we'll stop — no need to explain why. Any partner, employee, or customer can also request that their information be removed from our system entirely.

To opt out of marketing or request removal of your information, email us at support@sidecarservices.com and we'll take care of it.

Website Analytics & Security

Tools we use behind the scenes.

Like most business websites, sidecarservices.com uses a small set of standard third-party tools:

Google Analytics (GA4)

Helps us understand general site traffic — which pages get visited and how people find us — so we can make the site more useful. This data is aggregated and isn't used to identify individual visitors personally.

HubSpot

Powers our Free Evaluation form and stores the contact information submitted through it, as described above.

Cloudflare

Provides security and performance infrastructure for the site (protecting against bots and abuse) and does not collect personal information for marketing purposes.
