Common Questions

Apartment vending, answered.

Does our property have to pay for the machine or installation?

No — installation, the machine, stocking, and maintenance are all provided at no cost to your community.

Where do machines typically go in an apartment community?

Most commonly the leasing office lobby, clubhouse, or fitness center — we'll walk the property with you during your free evaluation to recommend the best spot for resident traffic.

Who handles complaints if something goes wrong?

Route them straight to us. A local Metro Atlanta technician responds the same day, so your leasing staff never has to get involved.

Can we request specific snacks or drinks for residents?

Yes, we build the initial product mix around your resident demographics and adjust it over time based on real sales data.
