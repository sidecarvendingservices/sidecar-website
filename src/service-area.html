Common Questions

Service area, answered.

How do I check if my exact address is covered?

Use the map above — click any highlighted zip code to zoom in, or scan the zip code list in the sidebar. If your zip code isn't listed, it's currently outside our service area.

Do you plan to expand outside these zip codes?

Service areas grow over time as demand increases. If your location isn't covered yet, request a free evaluation anyway — we'll follow up when coverage reaches your area.

Does being in the service area guarantee a machine gets installed?

Being in the service area means we can serve your location. Final placement still depends on your space and traffic, which we assess during your free on-site evaluation.
