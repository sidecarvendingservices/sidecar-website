Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about cost, contracts, service area, and what happens after you request a free evaluation.

Is it really free to get a vending machine installed?

Yes. Sidecar covers installation, the machine itself, ongoing stocking, and maintenance at no cost to your business. There's no capital outlay required to get started.

Do we have to sign a contract?

Yes — and it's designed to work in your favor. Before installation, locations sign a simple service agreement that's really our promise to keep your machine stocked, maintained, and running. You'll also get direct contact information for a dedicated representative who can help with any question or request, any time — not a call center. The only thing we ask in return is 30 days' notice if you ever need a machine relocated or removed. There's no capital outlay on your end — the agreement just puts our commitment to quality service in writing. See our how it works page for the full process.

What if we need to relocate or remove a machine later?

No problem — just give us 30 days' notice. That's the one thing we ask of locations under the service agreement, and it lets us plan the move or pickup without disrupting your space or ours.

What areas does Sidecar serve?

We serve businesses across North Metro Atlanta. Visit our service area page to see the full list of zip codes we currently cover, including an interactive map.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Offices and coworking spaces, warehouses and industrial facilities, apartment communities, gyms and fitness studios, medical and healthcare facilities, and auto dealerships. See our industries page for details specific to your type of space.

What payment methods do the machines accept?

All Sidecar machines are cashless-ready with touchscreens, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and card readers — no cash or exact change required.

How do you decide what to stock in the machine?

During your free on-site evaluation, we discuss your space and who's using it, then build a starting product mix. After that, smart inventory monitoring tracks what's actually selling so the mix keeps improving over time.

What happens if a machine breaks or runs out of stock?

Call us and a local Metro Atlanta technician responds the same day — not a national call center. Restocking is also based on real usage data, so machines are refilled before they run empty rather than on a fixed schedule.

How much space does a vending machine need?

It depends on the machine and your location, but most placements fit in a break room, lobby, or other high-traffic nook. We'll recommend the right size and spot during your free evaluation.

How do I get started?

Request your free on-site evaluation. A member of the Sidecar team will call or email you within 24 hours to schedule a visit — no cost, no pressure, no commitment required.

Is my location outside North Metro Atlanta still eligible?

Check our service area page for the current coverage map. If your zip code isn't listed yet, reach out anyway — service areas expand over time.
